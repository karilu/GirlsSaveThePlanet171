Food and Climate Change

Food and climate are two things we generally experience only passively. The goal of this project is for users to engage more meaningfully with trends of food production and the energy portfolios of various countries in hopes that they realize that as things are now, we are headed towards an increasingly unequal world.

In the following section we will present a short survey, the results of which will be used to generate starting filters for our first figure. There is no previous knowledge required and you are free to skip it if short on time.
